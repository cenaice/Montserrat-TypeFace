Montserrat

The oldest city in Buenos Aires

The Montserrat typeface is a geometric sans-serif typeface that was designed by graphic designer Julieta Ulanovsky. The typeface was released around 2011. It is designed with 18 various styles of font and 9 different weights. It is similar to the Proxima Nova, Gotham, Futura, Arial and Helvetica typefaces. The Montserrat typeface was worked around 2017 where it was redrawn by Jacques Le Bailly at Baron von Fonthausen.
